Recipe #2: Banana rolls

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
  • warm flour tortillas
  • chopped pecans
  • Plantains, sliced
  • Milk Spreadable
  • Corn oil for frying
  • 6
  • 1 cup
  • 2 1/2
  • 1 container

Step 1: To form the rolls, place a bit of walnut and banana on each tortilla, fold the edges inwards to prevent the filling from coming out, roll up and secure with toothpicks.
Step 2: Heat the oil and fry the rolls until golden brown. Place on absorbent paper to remove excess oil, remove the toothpicks and let cool slightly.
Step 3: Top the rolls with a little Leche Condensada; garnish with the walnuts and the remaining banana slices.
